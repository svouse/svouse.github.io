About Me

I'm a multimedia artist working with ceramic, kiln-cast glass, and novel view synthesis, a form of digital 3D visualization. My practice engages materiality, anonymity, repetition, and obfuscation as both method and subject.

I studied computer science at Oberlin College before completing my first artist residency at Pioneer Works in Spring 2023, returning for a second in Fall 2024. My work was featured in Peripheries Issue 6 (2024), an annual art and research journal from Harvard Divinity School that explores the "interstices between discourses, traditions, languages, forms, and genres."
