Methods

My Themes

Maximalism, information theory, archiving, and control are the central themes of my work.

I often think of my work as an extension of my body. You'll see cracks, leaks, scars, and structures I consider analogous to the cell. Some pieces are unstable and untrustworthy. Some, I destroy.
